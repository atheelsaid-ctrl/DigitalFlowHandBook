# Roles and Responsibilities

## Training Section Head

<details>

<summary>Responsibilities</summary>

* Define minimum skills
* Approve training plans
* Assign trainers
* Review test banks monthly
* Oversee annual verification
* Generate performance reports
* Escalate non-compliance cases

</details>

## Trainer

<details>

<summary>Responsibilities</summary>

* Deliver training (SS & PK)
* Track attendance daily
* Conduct evaluations
* Prepare verification exams
* Update test banks
* Submit weekly progress reports
* Monitor shadowing phase

</details>

## PM / AM

<details>

<summary>Responsibilities</summary>

* Raise hiring requests
* Define training dates
* Identify major/minor changes

</details>

## HR

<details>

<summary>Responsibilities</summary>

* Share trainee list 24 hours prior
* Confirm trainee headcount
* Handle failed / disqualified cases

</details>

## QA

<details>

<summary>Responsibilities</summary>

* Monitor shadowing agents
* Conduct certification
* Measure training quality KPI

</details>
