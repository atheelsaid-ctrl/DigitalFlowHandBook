# Overview

The Training Department at Digital Flow is responsible for:

* Defining minimum required skills per project
* Designing and delivering training programs
* Conducting verification and certification
* Monitoring trainee performance
* Maintaining training quality standards
* Managing refreshment & on-job training cycles
* Ensuring reporting compliance
