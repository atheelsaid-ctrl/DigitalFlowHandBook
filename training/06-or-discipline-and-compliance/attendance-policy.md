# Attendance Policy

## Absenteeism

* Max 2 days (training ≥ 2 weeks)
* Max 1 day (training < 2 weeks)

## Latency

* Max 2 late cases (≤ 1 hour)

{% hint style="danger" %}
Violation → Exclusion from training.
{% endhint %}
