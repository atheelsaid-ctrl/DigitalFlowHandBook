# Annual Reverification

## Trigger

12 months in same title.

## Completion Requirement

100% participation required.

## Benchmarks

| Score         | Action               |
| ------------- | -------------------- |
| > 95%         | Shadowing            |
| 85–95%        | Coaching             |
| < 85%         | Full re-verification |
| Repeated Fail | HR replacement       |
